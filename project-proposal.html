Project Proposal

  • What is your website about?
    • I am planning on making a wesite about Jordans. It will show all the Jordan models that have been released as well as the history behind some of the more significant shoes.

  • What is the objective of your website? (Also read as why would this site be important?)
    • The objective of this website will be to inform and educate those interested in Jordans. It will not only show you a the whole range of options available but also show the significance and history behind the iconic shoes.

  • Who is your target audience set?
    • My target audience will be amateur shoe collectors, sneakerheads, or just anyone trying to get more educated on Jordans.

  • What content do you plan to include in each page of your website?
    • I plan on one page showcasing all of the Jordan models that have been released to date with a brief description of the shoes. On the other pages I will dive deeper into the history behind some of the more iconic models and how they might have developed over time. Apart from just the history aspect of the shoes, I want to try to add a section about what colorways are the rarest for the models as well as talking about their street value/history.
